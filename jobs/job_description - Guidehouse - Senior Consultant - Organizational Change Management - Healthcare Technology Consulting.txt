Company: Guidehouse


Job Title: Senior Consultant - Organizational Change Management - Healthcare Technology Consulting



Guidehouse’s Health IT Solutions team partners with clients to improve technology outcomes through IT strategy, operational enhancements, and adoption of key technology initiatives. By leveraging proven health system IT best practices and data-driven insights, Guidehouse supports IT operational improvement and technology adoption across departments and service lines.

The Senior Consultant will support Organizational Change Management (OCM) efforts to drive successful adoption of Electronic Health Record (EHR) systems within complex healthcare environments. This role contributes to large-scale, government-sponsored EHR modernization initiatives by supporting the alignment of people, processes, and technology to enable effective and sustainable transformation.

What You Will Do:
Support the development and execution of change management strategies for EHR implementation projects

Assist with stakeholder engagement, communications, and training activities to promote user adoption and minimize resistance

Conduct organizational readiness assessments, workflow analyses, and impact assessments

Collaborate with clinical, operational, IT, and government stakeholders to support alignment with project goals and best practices

Contribute to the development of communication plans and training materials for diverse audiences

Track and report on change management activities, risks, and progress

Support continuous improvement efforts and knowledge sharing across project teams

What You Will Need:

Bachelor’s degree

Minimum of 3 years of experience in change management, healthcare IT, or large-scale transformation initiatives

Exposure to Organizational Change Management methodologies (e.g., PROSCI, CCMP) or relevant experience

Experience supporting at least one EHR implementation or similar transformation initiative

Basic understanding of clinical and operational workflows in hospital or health system settings

Strong communication and stakeholder engagement skills

Ability to manage multiple priorities in a fast-paced, dynamic environment

Willingness to travel up to 40%

U.S. citizenship is required

What Would Be Nice to Have:

Advanced degree in Public Health, Health Administration, Organizational Change Management, or Business Administration

Experience with Oracle Health (Cerner), Epic, MEDITECH, or other major EHR platforms

Experience supporting government or public health initiatives

Familiarity with behavioral health, pharmacy, or correctional health workflows
