Job Summary
We are seeking an energetic, driven professional who thrives in dynamic, client-facing
environments. The ideal candidate is a fast learner who can engage effectively with
stakeholders ranging from executives to frontline users.
As part of our central project team, the Implementation Specialist (Project Consultant) is
responsible for contributing to and executing the implementation of SaaS solutions for new
and existing clients.
This role operates within a team-based delivery model, where consultants collaborate to own
the end-to-end implementation while taking responsibility for specific components of project
execution. The position combines client-facing discovery, functional solution design, and
hands-on system configuration to support successful project outcomes.

This is a hands-on functional implementation role, not a pure project management position.
Success requires the ability to translate business requirements into system configurations,
contribute to delivery execution, and drive results alongside a broader project team.

Essential Functions & Responsibilities
Client Discovery & Requirements Translation
• Lead client discovery sessions to understand business processes, workflows, and
operational requirements
• Translate business needs into functional system configurations and solution designs
• Act as a key bridge between client stakeholders and internal technical teams
Implementation & Configuration
• Contribute to the team-based configuration and implementation of system
components, including:
• Master data setup
• User roles and permissions
• Functional modules and workflows
• Take a hands-on role in configuring and validating system setup as part of the project
team
• Own assigned portions of configuration and implementation workstreams, ensuring
accuracy and quality
Delivery Execution & Ownership
• Take ownership of assigned project components within a broader team-led
implementation
• Collaborate with team members to collectively deliver successful implementations

• Drive assigned workstreams to completion, ensuring deliverables are completed on
time, within scope, and at a high standard
• Maintain accountability for the quality, accuracy, and completeness of assigned
deliverables
Cross-Functional Collaboration
• Collaborate closely with Product, Development, QA, and Professional Services teams

reporting, integrations)

capabilities
Testing, Training & Post-Go-Live Support

functionality
• Lead or support client training sessions tailored to business processes and system
usage

Documentation & Continuous Improvement
• Create and maintain project documentation, configurations, and process flows

Project Scoping & Planning
