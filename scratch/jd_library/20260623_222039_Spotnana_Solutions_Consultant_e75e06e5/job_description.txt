Who: You! And the rest of the Sales department and cross-functional partners across Product, Account Management, and Implementation.

What: A Solutions Consultant position and an outstanding ability to build trust through consultative customer engagement.


Why: As a Solutions Consultant, you will play a critical role in helping strategic partners and enterprise customers understand the transformational value of Spotnana's modern travel infrastructure. You will lead discovery discussions, executive presentations, solution demonstrations, and strategic engagements that help customers envision a better future for travel. This role is instrumental in supporting one of Spotnana's most important growth channels while delivering a differentiated customer experience throughout the buying journey.


The day-to-day:
Lead discovery workshops with enterprise customers and strategic partners.
Deliver executive presentations, solution demonstrations, and transformation discussions that connect business challenges to Spotnana's platform capabilities.
Support strategic partner sales teams throughout complex sales cycles and help develop business cases and value propositions for enterprise opportunities.
Collaborate closely with Product, Account Management, Implementation, and Solution Engineering teams to support customer success and solution adoption.
Support RFP responses, competitive evaluations, executive presentations, and strategic customer engagements.
Represent Spotnana at customer meetings, industry events, and partner engagements while providing market feedback on customer needs and competitive trends.

Skills & qualities we value:
7+ years of experience in enterprise software sales engineering, solutions consulting, strategic consulting, enterprise sales, or a related customer-facing role.
Exceptional communication, presentation, and consultative discovery skills with the ability to engage executive business stakeholders and technical audiences.
Experience leading complex enterprise customer conversations and connecting technology capabilities to measurable business outcomes.
Experience working with strategic partners, channels, or ecosystem-driven sales motions.
Strong executive presence with the ability to navigate ambiguity and align multiple stakeholders around a common vision.
Experience operating successfully within fast-growing, high-change environments.
Travel industry experience.
