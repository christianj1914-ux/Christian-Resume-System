Job Title: Sales Solutions Consultant





At Twin Health, we empower people to improve and prevent chronic metabolic diseases, like type 2 diabetes and obesity, with a new standard of care. Twin Health is the only company applying AI Digital Twin technology exclusively toward metabolic health. 

We start by building a dynamic model of each person’s metabolism — drawing on thousands of data points from CGMs, smartwatches, and meal logs — that maps their personal path to better health. Guided by a dedicated clinical care team, our members have lowered their A1C below the diabetes range, achieved lasting weight loss, and reduced or even eliminated medications, all while living healthier, happier lives.

Opportunity

We’re seeking a Sales Solutions Consultant to serve as a critical bridge between the commercial and cross-functional sides of the business. In this role, you’ll partner closely with the Sales, Marketing, Clinical, Product, Analytics, and Operations teams to design and deliver strategic, solutions-oriented materials that clearly communicate how Twin’s platform meets the needs of our clients. You’ll play a key role in structuring proposals, campaigns, sales collateral, and customized solutions for health plans, employers, brokers, unions, and provider organizations.

Responsibilities

Partner with the Commercial team to develop winning strategies and accelerate enterprise growth

Conduct market, prospect, and stakeholder research to inform deal strategy and uncover opportunities
Design tailored solution strategies that align Twin's capabilities with prospect priorities 
Translate complex clinical, product, and operational concepts into compelling executive-level narratives, presentations, and business cases
Leverage AI, automation, and analytics to improve commercial effectiveness
Collaborate across Product, Clinical, Operations, and Marketing teams to shape market-facing strategy and messaging
Participate in cross-functional planning for pilots, strategic initiatives, and enterprise implementations
Drive operational excellence by improving commercial processes, CRM adoption, pipeline visibility, and reporting
Maintain a high bar for quality, consistency, and rigor across all deliverables
