Job Title: Professional Services Consultant



Job Type: Contractor



Location: Remote



Job Summary: In this role, you'll apply your expertise to help train next-generation AI systems. Your work will shape how models learn, reason, and perform through high-quality, real-world input.



Key Responsibilities:

• Leverage your deep domain expertise as a Software Developer, Management Consultant, Corporate Lawyer / Legal Counsel, or Certified Public Accountant (CPA) / Auditor to provide critical, real-world input.

• Draft, evaluate, and revise strategic slide decks, contract risk assessments, and financial audit reports to ensure data accuracy and professional insight.

• Deliver clear, comprehensive written and verbal feedback on complex scenarios and project materials.

• Collaborate closely with the customer's team and other expert consultants to ensure alignment with project milestones and objectives.

• Analyze and clarify domain-specific data to support the high-quality training of AI models.

• Participate in expert reviews to uphold industry standards and identify areas for improvement.

• Commit a minimum of 10+ hours per week to deliver high-impact results.



Required Skills and Qualifications:

• Extensive experience as a consultant, lawyer, auditor, or developer (minimum 5-10+ years preferred).

• Exceptional English communication skills, both written and verbal, with a keen eye for detail.

• Advanced proficiency in preparing and reviewing strategic slide decks, contracts, risk documentation, or end-of-year (EOY) financial audit reports.

• Strong analytical skills and the ability to synthesize complex concepts into clear guidance.

• Demonstrated ability to collaborate in distributed teams and adapt to evolving project requirements.

• Proven record of delivering high-quality, expert-level work on deadline.

• Technologically adept, with the ability to quickly learn and utilize online collaboration tools.



Preferred Qualifications:

• Based in the US, Canada, UK, Europe, or LATAM with a strong record of cross-functional teamwork.

• Prior experience contributing to professional services projects or consulting for enterprise clients.

• Experience working in remote, fast-paced, or AI-adjacent environments is a plus.
