Company: Acrisure


Job Title: Solutions Architect





About Acrisure

A global fintech leader, Acrisure empowers millions of ambitious businesses and individuals with the right solutions to grow boldly forward. Bringing cutting-edge technology and top-tier human support together, we connect clients with customized solutions across a range of insurance, reinsurance, payroll, benefits, cybersecurity, mortgage services, and more. 

In the last twelve years, Acrisure has grown in revenue from $38 million to almost $5 billion and employs over 19,000 colleagues in more than 20 countries. Acrisure was built on entrepreneurial spirit. Prioritizing leadership, accountability, and collaboration, we equip our teams to work at the highest levels possible.

Job Summary:

The Solutions Architect is a hands-on, execution-focused role within the Transformation Office, designed to accelerate Acrisure’s shift toward a more AI-enabled, digitally mature enterprise. This role partners closely with business and technology teams to design, prototype, and implement scalable solutions that improve efficiency, enhance data visibility, and enable automation across the organization. 

This position reflects a strategic evolution toward modern talent—prioritizing individuals who are deeply tech-savvy, AI-forward, and builder-oriented—capable of driving meaningful outcomes without reliance on heavy leadership overhead.

The Solutions Architect will play a critical role in advancing enterprise transformation initiatives that support an evolution into a technology-enabled, AI-driven organization, focused on operational rigor, scalability, and long-term public company readiness. 

Key Responsibilities:

AI-Forward Solutioning & Innovation 
Identify and implement opportunities for AI, automation, and workflow optimization across business functions 
Apply structured analysis to define scalable, AI-enabled solution requirements, ensuring traceability from business objectives through technical delivery while aligning to enterprise architecture, data models, and integration standards 
Leverage modern tools (AI-assisted development, low-code/no-code platforms, data tooling) to accelerate delivery 
Rapidly prototype and iterate on solutions 
Business & Technology Alignment 
Lead the creation and documentation of current and future state end-to-end process flows, business workflows, and requirements, translating complex business needs into clear, actionable artifacts (e.g., user stories, functional specs, solution designs) that enable efficient build and implementation 
Translate business needs into scalable technical solutions 
Serve as a bridge between business, data, and technology stakeholders 
Enterprise Transformation Delivery 
Support enterprise initiatives including M&A integration and platform rationalization 
Contribute to intake and prioritization processes 
Data & Scalability Enablement 
Promote data visibility and governance 
Design solutions aligned with public company expectations 
Cross-Functional Collaboration 
Partner across Transformation, IT, and business units 
Education and Experience:

Bachelor’s degree (or higher) in Information Technology or related field 
3–7 years of experience 
Experience with transformation initiatives 
AI/analytics exposure 
Risk/compliance experience is a plus 
Technical curiosity and builder mindset 
Experience with AI-enabled tools and automation 
Proven ability to create rapport with and influence all levels of stakeholders 
Strong communication skills and personal credibility; can comfortably lead senior executives through difficult discussions and creatively drive consensus 
Low-ego and high EQ, while being equally comfortable giving and receiving direction, leading and contributing, driving executive conversations or supporting as necessary, ability to 'read the room' and adjust approach, and foster trust at levels of the organization 
Proven experience defining and documenting detailed business and technical requirements, along with workflows and process flows, ensuring business needs are translated into scalable, tech-enabled solutions with traceability from concept through delivery.
