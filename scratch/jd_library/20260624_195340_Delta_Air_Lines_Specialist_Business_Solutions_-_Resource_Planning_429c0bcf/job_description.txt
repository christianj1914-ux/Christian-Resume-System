Company: Delta Air Lines


Job Title: Specialist, Business Solutions - Resource Planning


How you'll help us Keep Climbing (overview & key responsibilities)


At Delta, we are transforming how we deliver exceptional customer experiences across Reservations and Customer Care—with resource planning at the center of that evolution.

As a Specialist, Business Solutions – Resource Planning, you will play a critical role in optimizing how we forecast, plan, and deploy our workforce of 6,000+ Delta specialists and 2,000+ 3rd party business partners. This role is ideal for those who thrive at the intersection of forecasting, analytics, and operational strategy, and is ready to influence decision-making.

You will leverage advanced analytics to improve forecast accuracy, close coverage gaps, and drive data-informed solutions that balance service level performance, productivity, and employee experience. This role enables you to deepen your business acumen while advancing data-driven workforce management strategies.

Key Responsibilities:

Apply forecasting and analysis expertise to inform strategic decisions impacting resources and operational coverage
Champion a continuous optimization mindset by analyzing trends, measuring outcomes, and driving iterative improvements in forecasting accuracy and resource utilization
Collaborate cross-functionally to advance strategic workforce management initiatives, aligning resource deployment with evolving business priorities
Proactively identify coverage risks and inefficiencies and build dynamic “what-if” models to guide mitigation strategies
Deliver data-driven staffing guidance to third-party business partners to proactively close coverage gaps and optimize operational performance
Design a streamlined suite of comprehensive reporting and visualizations highlighting insights for stakeholders
Thrive in a high-paced environment, demonstrating the ability to manage multiple priorities


What you need to succeed (minimum qualifications)

Candidates for this position should have at minimum a bachelor’s degree in business, technology, or a related field preferred or with 4-6 years of relevant analytical work experience
Proficiency in Tableau or Power BI and Microsoft Office (ie Excel, PowerPoint, etc)
Intellectual curiosity with strong ability to interpret complex data and translate it into actionable insights
Advanced proficiency in SAS/SQL or similar tools with the ability to write complex queries
Excellent verbal and written communication skills, with attention to detail and the ability to present data-driven insights to stakeholders
Strong ability to collaborate with stakeholders from other departments and ability to develop effective relationships with all levels
Self-starter with strong prioritization skills and an ability to manage priorities under short deadlines
Must be performing satisfactorily in current position
Consistently prioritizes safety and security of self, others, and personal data
Embraces diverse people, thinking, and styles
Possesses a high school diploma, GED, or high school equivalency
Is at least 18 years of age and has authorization to work in the United States


What will give you a competitive edge (preferred qualifications)

Contact Center knowledge including resource planning, processes, tools and infrastructure
Bachelor’s degree in Data Science, Statistics, Computer Science, or Business Analytics
