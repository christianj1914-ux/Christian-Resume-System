Company: Delta Air Lines


Job Title: Product Manager, Airport Customer Service


How you'll help us Keep Climbing (overview & key responsibilities)
The Product Manager, Airport Customer Service will help provide Delta's 30,000+ Airport Customer Service frontline team with the necessary technology and tools to offer customers a warm, caring, and elevated travel experience. We are looking for a highly motivated and self-driven Product Manager to join our team to support the technology conceptualization and product delivery of best-in-class tools to serve our frontline teams effectively. As part of the Tools and Technology team, you will play a critical role in redefining customer and employee experiences by setting new technology standards for thousands of ACS employees. We are seeking a Product Manager to support product development and improvements from ideation, roadmapping to execution and adoption. 
This role reports to the Resource Optimization Manager in ACS Tools & Technology, based at the General Office in Atlanta.  
PRIMARY FUNCTIONS: 
Requirements Definition: Gathering of business requirements from Strategy and operational stakeholders to translate into product features 
Prioritization and Roadmap Definition: Balancing ever-changing business priorities with a focused IT development plan 
Implementation: Partner with Strategy and Learning teams to oversee change management and the launch of new products or features  
Post-Launch Optimization: provide strong product support to ensure operational readiness and robustness 

What you need to succeed (minimum qualifications)
Must be an excellent communicator, able to liaise seamlessly between business & technical teams, working groups, frontline, and senior leaders 
Comfortable working in a fast-paced environment, handling multiple priorities with minimal oversight, and working effectively with cross-functional technical and business teams 
Translate product strategy and business needs into executable product roadmaps, functional requirements, and sequenced initiatives 
Contribute to full product support and lifecycle, including growth, optimization, and retirement phases in partnership with IT 
Partner with IT to ensure delivery as scoped and on-time 
Operationalize defined success metrics & monitor product performance against KPIs 
Maintain heavy communication with frontline and leader stakeholders for feature development, alignment on priorities, and continuous product support 
Must put safety first in all product design and implementation decisions 
Monitor adoption, reliability, and ROI 
Partner with Strategy and Experience teams to incorporate defined personas and journey insights into roadmap refinement 
Moderate Domestic US travel may be required 

What will give you a competitive edge (preferred qualifications)
Experience participating in cost estimates, impact analyses, and business case development
Experience working in baggage or below wing operations
Comfortable working with all levels of the organization, from frontline to senior leadership
Experience with product launches and change management in an operation setting
Familiarization with competitive markets/technologies and incorporating trends or best practices into roadmaps or prioritization decisions
