Company: Delta Air Lines


Job Title: Project Manager, Crew Mod Implementation

How you'll help us Keep Climbing (overview & key responsibilities)
As the Project Manager for Crew Modernization (Crew Mod) initiatives, you will lead the implementation of strategic transformation efforts across Crew Resources, supporting core Crew Mod products including profile, scheduling, rotation management, and training. You will partner with the Crew Mod strategy team to transition initiatives from long-term design into execution, owning delivery through implementation, adoption, and operational stabilization.
This role is responsible for driving the successful deployment of complex, cross-functional initiatives that modernize crew systems and processes. You will ensure all aspects of implementation readiness are executed effectively, including testing, change management, communications, and training.
You will operate as a business-focused implementation leader, working across product, operations, and technology teams to translate strategy into outcomes that improve efficiency, user experience, and operational reliability. The role requires coordination across multiple product areas and supporting systems, including legacy platforms, and partnership with a diverse set of teams such as BIPA and other initiative groups to ensure aligned and cohesive delivery.
This position reports to the Manager of Crew Technology and PMO and plays a critical role in enabling the successful rollout and adoption of Crew Mod initiatives across the operation.
