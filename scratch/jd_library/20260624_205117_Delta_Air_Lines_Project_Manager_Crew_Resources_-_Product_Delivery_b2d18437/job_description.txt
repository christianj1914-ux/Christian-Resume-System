Job Title: Project Manager, Crew Resources - Product Delivery


How you'll help us Keep Climbing (overview & key responsibilities)

This role ensures the right conversations happen at the right time, decisions are documented, owners are clearly assigned, and deliverables move forward with discipline and clarity.

The Project Manager creates the structure, visibility, and communication cadence that enable the Product Owner, IT teams, and business partners to operate effectively.

This position will be based in the Atlanta General Offices and report to the Manager – Business Insights AI/ML.
