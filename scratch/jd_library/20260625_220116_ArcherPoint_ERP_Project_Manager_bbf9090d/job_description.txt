Company: ArcherPoint 


Job Title: ERP Project Manager
 

About the Role
ArcherPoint is hiring an ERP Project Manager to lead Microsoft Dynamics 365 Business Central implementations for mid-market clients. In this role, you’ll own the full project lifecycle—from kickoff through go-live—working closely with client stakeholders, internal solution architects, and functional consultants to deliver on time, on scope, and on budget. This is a highly visible, client-facing role that requires equal parts structure and adaptability.

About ArcherPoint
ArcherPoint, a Cherry Bekaert company, is a Microsoft Dynamics partner with deep expertise in Business Central implementations. We’re not a typical consulting firm—our team operates with a strong sense of ownership and accountability that shapes everything from how we serve clients to how we support each other. We work in a collaborative, fast-moving environment where your contributions directly shape the company’s future.

What You’ll Do
Lead end-to-end ERP project delivery using agile frameworks and sprint-based methodologies, from project initiation through go-live and hypercare.
Build and maintain project plans, sprint plans, risk registers, and lessons-learned documentation throughout the engagement.
Facilitate client workshops, sprint reviews, retrospectives, and executive steering committee meetings with clarity and confidence.
Manage scope, change requests, and contract adherence—escalating risks early and proposing practical mitigation strategies.
Collaborate with the Resource Manager and practice leads to ensure the right expertise is assigned at the right time.
Serve as a servant leader to your project team—fostering psychological safety, coaching team members, and maintaining morale through the peaks and valleys of a long-haul implementation.
Translate technical concepts for non-technical stakeholders and business requirements for technical teams.
Maintain strong project health metrics and provide transparent, timely reporting to internal leadership and clients.
What You Bring
Required

5+ years of experience managing complex ERP implementations in mid-market environments
Demonstrated ability to manage client relationships at the executive level
Strong command of agile project management principles and sprint-based delivery
Proficiency in monday.com for project tracking and team coordination
Proficiency in Microsoft Office (Excel, PowerPoint, Word, Teams)
Willingness to travel to client sites, occasionally on short notice
Preferred

PMP, CSM, SAFe, or equivalent project management or agile certification
Hands-on experience with Microsoft Dynamics NAV or Dynamics 365 Business Central
Familiarity with ISV solutions (e.g., LS Central, Boltrics) or other Business Central extensions
What Success Looks Like in Your First 90 Days

You’re actively managing project health, flagging risks proactively, and running sprint cadences with confidence.
Your team trusts you—and your clients view you as a steady, capable partner.
