Company: ArcherPoint 


Job Title: Implementation Consultant
 

ArcherPoint is currently adding a LS Retail Implementation Consultant to support our rapidly expanding Retail practice. This Retail practice area is focused on the LS Retail solution built on Microsoft Dynamics NAV, so experience with both platforms is preferred however deep experience with Dynamics NAV is required. The ideal candidate for this role will have 5+ years of experience working with clients to prepare and develop high-level solutions to meet their business requirements. Candidates must also have hands-on experience with implementations. Candidates with experience working with LS One are a plus! This role will specifically be accountable for:

Applying Retail and POS expertise and knowledge to assist in the identification, sales, and delivery of Retail/POS Solutions 
Eliciting Retail and POS related requirements from stakeholders 
Analyzing prospect and client Retail and POS business requirements 
Preparing and developing high-level solutions to meet business requirements 
Translating solution components into functional requirements, tasks, activities, and conditions of satisfaction 
Configuring Retail and POS system setup based upon business requirements 
Making recommendations for future Retail and POS enhancements to meet changing Retail/POS industry and specific business needs 

In addition to the above accountabilities & requirements, the successful candidate for this role must align well with our very defined, active, and dynamic organizational culture. We are a company of employees with a passion to help solve customer problems in a collaborative dynamic environment and we seek team members, that are ready for a significant change and the opportunity to help shape our company today and help us build the future.
