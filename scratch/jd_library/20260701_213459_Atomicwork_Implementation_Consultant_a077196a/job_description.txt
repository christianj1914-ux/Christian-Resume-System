Company: Atomicwork




Job Title: Enterprise Solution Consultant



Atomicwork is on a mission to transform the digital workplace experience by uniting people, processes, and platforms through AI workforce. Our team is building a modern service management platform that enables growing businesses to reduce operational complexity and drive business success.


Enterprise Solution Consultant:

We’re looking for an experienced Solution Consultant to join our team and help customers evaluate, design, and adopt Atomicwork’s AI Workforce and service management capabilities. This role is open to remote candidates, with a strong preference for candidates based in the San Francisco Bay Area.


What we’re looking for (qualifications)

We are not big on formal qualifications, and we value hands-on skills. We also enjoy working with people who want to learn new things and have a sense of responsibility toward their work.
5+ years of experience in a pre-sales / solutions consulting role
Hands-on exposure to ITSM, enterprise automation, and enterprise AI is preferred
Experience designing end-to-end AI-powered workflows, including defining agent behavior, selecting tools and integrations, incorporating human-in-the-loop for edge cases, and shaping demos for technical or security stakeholders.
Familiarity with MCP or similar integration frameworks, and confidence explaining how AI agents securely connect to enterprise data, applications, and APIs.
Willingness to travel within the United States as necessary.


What you’ll do (responsibilities)

Own the technical strategy for sales opportunities, partnering with account teams and prospects throughout the evaluation process.
Lead discovery conversations that connect customer challenges, AI strategy, and service operations priorities to clear business outcomes and value realization.
Design and present technical solutions that show how Atomicwork’s AI Workforce, service management capabilities, and integrations can address prospect needs in a scalable way.
Collaborate with prospects, partners, and internal teams to address technical questions, shape solution architectures, and capture product and market feedback.
Drive proof-of-concept engagements and success criteria that build confidence, demonstrate measurable value, and support forward momentum in the sales cycle.
Help build the Solution Consulting function by contributing to new GTM motions, team best practices, reusable assets, and cross-functional initiatives.


Why we are different (culture)

As a part of Atomicwork, you can shape our company and business from idea to production. Our cultural values also set the bar high, helping us create a better workplace for everyone.
Autonomy: We champion self-direction to deliver customer success, empowering teams and individuals to deliver peak performance.
Trust: We unwaveringly believe in our colleagues' positive intentions, approaching every interaction with trust to accelerate execution.
Ownership: We demonstrate unwavering commitment to our mission and goals, taking full responsibility for triumphs and setbacks.
Mastery: We relentlessly pursue continuous self-improvement as individuals and teams, dedicating ourselves to constant learning and growth.
Impatience: We recognize that our world moves swiftly and is driven by an unyielding desire to progress with every endeavor.
Customer Obsession: We place our customers at the heart of everything we do, relentlessly seeking to understand their needs and exceed their expectations.
