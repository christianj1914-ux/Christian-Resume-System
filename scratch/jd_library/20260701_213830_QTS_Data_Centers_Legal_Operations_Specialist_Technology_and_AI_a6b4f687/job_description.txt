Who You Are:

The Legal Technology and AI Specialist will support the development, implementation and optimization of legal technology and AI-enabled solutions across the Legal team. This role partners with each Legal division, IT, and business stakeholders to improve workflows, increase efficiency, and scale legal service delivery.

This position operates as an individual contributor within a defined career band and contributes to business outcomes through process improvement, solution delivery, technology enablement, and successful AI adoption and implementation. Success will be measured by increased adoption of legal technology and AI solutions, effective implementation of AI-enabled capabilities, improved cycle times, and quantifiable gains in operational efficiency and overall effectiveness.

What You Will Do:

Support implementation, configuration, and ongoing optimization of legal technology platforms and AI tools, including Microsoft 365 Copilot and Copilot Studio.

Develop and maintain AI-enabled workflows and Copilot agents in alignment with governance and security standards

Translate business needs into requirements, process flows, and scalable solutions

Identify and execute process improvements to enhance efficiency, quality, and consistency across legal operations

Partner with cross-functional teams (Legal, IT, vendors) to support design, testing (UAT), deployment, and stabilization of solutions

Support responsible AI practices, including data handling, access controls, and risk mitigation

Develop and deliver training, playbooks, and enablement resources to support adoption and effective use of tools

Track and report on adoption, usage, and performance metrics; recommend continuous improvements

Evaluate emerging technologies and contribute to tool selection and implementation decisions

What You Will Need to be Successful:

Bachelor’s degree in information systems, computer science, business, or related field, or equivalent experience
Experience supporting or implementing business applications in a professional, legal, or regulated environment
Ability to translate business processes into workflows, requirements, and technical documentation
Technical understanding of generative AI architectures, prompt engineering, and enterprise use cases.
Working knowledge of generative AI concepts and practical applications
Experience supporting solutions across the full project management lifecycle (planning, testing, implementation, rollout, support)
Experience with Microsoft 365 Copilot and/or Copilot Studio
Experience with Microsoft Power Platform (e.g., Power Automate, Power Apps)
Experience with Claude
Experience applying AI to automate workflows and drive process improvements

Exposure to legal technology tools and supporting legal teams
